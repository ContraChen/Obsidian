**简介：**
该插件可让在 Obsidian 中创建交互式图表
**使用方法：**
![](https://pic2.zhimg.com/v2-3b1691ef8a2af57b89d855ca6f3268cd_b.jpg)
**实例：**
```chart
	type: bar
	labels: [11,22,33,44,55]
	series:
		- title: t1
		data: [1,2,3,4,5]
		- title: t2
		data: [1,2,3,4,5]
```
